Subteam Leaders

Leo Nhuch – Mechanical Lead

Leo leads our mechanical subteam, overseeing the design, fabrication, and assembly of all robot components. He coordinates manufacturing and mentors team members in workshop safety and fabrication techniques.

Nico Rossini – Programming Lead

Nico heads our programming subteam, developing autonomous routines and driver control systems using Java. He manages code organization, sensors, and testing for reliable performance.

Luca Notti – Design Lead

Luca leads CAD design and prototyping using Fusion 360, ensuring all parts are optimized for performance, fit, and manufacturability before fabrication.

Jackson Beach – Outreach Lead

Jackson organizes community events, demonstrations, and social media outreach to promote STEM education and increase the team’s visibility.

Mr. Adams – Mentor

Mr. Adams provides technical guidance, safety oversight, and engineering mentorship to support student growth and team success.

Every member plays a critical role in building a competitive and well-rounded FTC robot.
