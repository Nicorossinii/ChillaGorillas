Who We Are

The Chilla Gorillas are a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team from San Rafael High School in California. We design, build, and program competitive robots while developing engineering, leadership, and teamwork skills.

FTC DECODE

In the DECODE season, teams are challenged to solve complex engineering problems through robot design, autonomous coding, and strategic gameplay.

Engineering Skills

Our team gains hands-on experience in CAD, fabrication, Java programming, control systems, and iterative design.

Community Impact

We promote STEM education through outreach, mentorship, and demonstrations in our local community.

Proudly Representing San Rafael High School

As students of San Rafael High School, we strive to represent our school with excellence, professionalism, and innovation both on and off the competition field.

From kickoff to competition day, we document our progress, challenges and successes throughout the FTC season.
