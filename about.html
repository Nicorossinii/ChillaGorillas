Chilla Gorillas is a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team dedicated to innovation, collaboration, and learning through hands-on engineering.

Competing in the FTC DECODE season, we tackle challenges involving robot design, autonomous programming, driver control, and strategic gameplay.

Guided by the FIRST values of Gracious Professionalism® and Coopertition®, we strive to grow as engineers and leaders.
