Robotics Projects

Robot Assembly

Our team collaborated to assemble the main robot chassis, integrating mechanical components with electronic systems. This process involved precise alignment of drive motors, mounting of structural elements, and installation of control systems to create a robust competition-ready robot.

CAD Design Process

Using Fusion 360, our design team created detailed 3D models of robot components. We went through multiple iterations to optimize part geometry, ensure proper fit and clearance, and simulate mechanical performance before fabrication.

Programming Development

Our programming team developed autonomous routines and driver control systems using Java. We implemented sensor integration, path planning algorithms, and control loops to enable precise robot movement and task execution during competitions.

Mechanical Fabrication

In our workshop, team members fabricated custom parts using various manufacturing techniques including CNC milling, 3D printing, and manual machining. These components were essential for creating specialized mechanisms unique to our competition strategy.

Testing and Iteration

Continuous testing and refinement was crucial for robot performance. We conducted extensive field testing, analyzed performance data, and made iterative improvements to mechanical systems, software algorithms, and control strategies to optimize competition results.
