Subteam Leaders

Leo Nhuch - Mechanical Lead

Leo leads our mechanical subteam, overseeing the design, fabrication, and assembly of all robot components. He coordinates the manufacturing process, ensures quality control, and mentors team members in workshop safety and fabrication techniques. Under his leadership, the mechanical team has developed innovative mechanisms for competition success.

Nico Rossini - Programming Lead

Nico heads our programming subteam, developing autonomous routines and driver control systems using Java. He implements sensor integration, path planning algorithms, and control loops to enable precise robot movement. Nico also manages version control and code documentation to ensure reliable software performance.

Luca Notti - Design Lead

Luca leads our design subteam, creating advanced CAD prototypes and iterations using Fusion 360. He oversees the entire design process from concept to final production, ensuring all components are optimized for performance and manufacturability. Luca's expertise in 3D modeling and simulation has been crucial for our robot's success.

Jackson Beach - Outreach Lead

Jackson directs our outreach initiatives, encouraging community engagement and sharing team events. He organizes demonstrations, manages social media presence, and coordinates STEM education programs. Jackson's efforts have significantly increased our team's visibility and impact in the local community.

Mr. Adams - Mentor

Mr. Adams provides invaluable technical expertise and guidance to all team members. With his extensive engineering background, he mentors students in advanced concepts, safety protocols, and professional practices. His support has been instrumental in developing our team's technical capabilities and professional growth.

Every member plays a critical role in building a competitive and well-rounded FTC robot.
